SAN ESIM(NUMBERS)

Numerical noun is a word group that expresses the number, size, sequence, magnitude of an object. Since the number noun is the name of abstract numerical concepts when spoken individually, their real meaning is determined when communicating with other words, in the process of defining them numerically.
